Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Introduction

SchemeWise ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the SchemeWise mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Profile Data: Age, gender, state, district, caste category, income, occupation, and other demographic details you provide to receive scheme recommendations.
  • Usage Data: Schemes you view, save, or apply for within the app.
  • Device Information: Device type and OS version for app compatibility purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with eligible government schemes based on your profile.
  • To send email verification and account-related notifications.
  • To improve app performance and scheme matching accuracy.
  • To provide personalised scheme recommendations and updates.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only used within SchemeWise to provide the service.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to store sensitive information. Your data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely in our database.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time. See our Data Deletion page for instructions.

Children's Privacy

SchemeWise is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
