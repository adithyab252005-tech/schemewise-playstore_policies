SchemeWise — Account & Data Deletion Request

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account credentials (email and password)
  • Your personal profile (name, age, state, income, category, etc.)
  • Your saved schemes and application history
  • All eligibility data associated with your account
⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

How to Request Deletion

1

Send an email to schemewise.in@gmail.com

2

Use subject line: Account Data Deletion Request

3

Include your registered email address in the message body so we can identify your account.

4

We will process your request within 7 business days and send a confirmation email.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at schemewise.in@gmail.com
