Request Data Deletion

If you have used the SchemeWise application and would like your account and any stored data to be permanently deleted, you may request it at any time.

How to Request Deletion

1. Send an email to: schemewise.support@example.com

2. Set the subject line to: Account Data Deletion Request

3. Include your registered email address or mobile number so we can identify your account.

We will process your request and permanently delete your information within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.
